# 0.8.0 — 2026-06-24

The Cue Card becomes the whole live surface, mock turns into a real copilot
rehearsal, Reports gets a clean table, and a pile of polish: cost-effective
models, sample data, a fuller tour, and stealth options.

## Added

- **The Cue Card is now your full live surface.** During an interview the floating
  panel shows the **live transcript** (resizable) above the streamed answer, plus
  everything you need without the dashboard: **Pause/Stop**, **Solve from clipboard**
  and **Select region**, a **mic-level meter**, an **Ask** box for manual questions,
  and a ⚙ **settings** modal to pick your microphone. It still hides from screen
  sharing.
- **Live answer controls.** Change **Interview Type**, **Answer Format**, and
  **Answer Length** on the fly, toggle **pronunciation hints**, and **Regenerate** or
  **Clear** an answer — all from the Cue Card, mid-interview.
- **Pronunciation hints** render in a simple, readable form, e.g.
  *regulations (reg-yuh-LAY-shunz)*.
- **Save or discard at the end.** When you stop, BrainCue asks whether to **save the
  session** (you pick what kind of interview it was) or **discard** it — nothing is
  kept unless you choose to.
- **Sample data, in one click.** Profiles → **Load sample data** seeds a sample
  résumé and three ready interviews (Google, Amazon, Stripe) so you can try the whole
  flow immediately.
- **Practice with sample questions.** On the Interview page, **play a sample
  interviewer question aloud** to test the live Cue Card end-to-end on your own.
- **Hide from the taskbar.** A new stealth toggle (Settings → Privacy Mode) keeps the
  app off the taskbar; it stays reachable from the tray and Cue Card.
- **Guided tour, expanded** — a clearer walkthrough of the full workflow: key →
  profile → interview → Cue Card → save → mock → reports → privacy.

## Changed

- **"Live Session" is now "Interview."** The page lists your interviews in a
  searchable table; press **Start** (or **Resume**) on a row — the live experience
  opens in the Cue Card, so you can minimize the dashboard. Interview **type is now
  switched live** in the Cue Card (a single interview can cover behavioral *and*
  coding), and each question is tagged with its type.
- **Mock interview = a copilot rehearsal.** The AI interviewer asks questions aloud
  and the copilot answers them in the Cue Card — a true test of the live experience.
  Mock runs aren't saved to Reports.
- **Reports, simplified.** A clean, paginated, searchable **table** of sessions
  (client · type · profile · date), with a one-click coaching report. Each session
  shows its question-type breakdown.
- **Cost-effective by default.** Default models moved to the efficient tiers
  (answers on `gpt-4.1-mini`, classification on `gpt-4.1-nano`) for much lower
  per-session cost. Upgrade any task in **Settings → OpenAI Models**, now with a
  proper scrollable model picker.
- **Removing a résumé or JD cleans up after itself** — its indexed data is removed
  too, so retrieval never uses stale content.
- **Reset anytime.** Need a clean slate? **Settings → Danger zone** lets you reset all
  settings, or remove all data (your key, profiles, interviews, and sessions).
- A lighter, more attractive UI pass: themed scrollbars, smooth page transitions, and
  refined hover/focus states.

## Fixed

- **Deleting an interview no longer errors** ("FOREIGN KEY constraint failed") — its
  job description, indexed data, and links are cleaned up correctly.
- The audio meter now moves only when someone is actually speaking, instead of
  twitching on background noise.
